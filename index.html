WHAT IS OUR GOAL?

To help in the management of scattered labour sector

The Indian labour industry is tremendously saturated, with the majority of employees being skilled or semi-skilled.

We continue to contribute to the solution of this problem by establishing connections between contractors and employees, as well as educating people about government programmes.

This also aids in lowering the burden on the government to assist the underprivileged.

Problems faced by the workers

The workers encounter a variety of issues based on a variety of causes.

Half-time work

Because the majority of the labour is done solely during the day, the workers are idle the rest of the day.

No second source of income

Because the workers are unoccupied most of the daytime and are unaware of available jobs, there is no secondary source of income.

Unawareness

The majority of workers are uninformed of the government's plans for them and hence fail to enjoy the greatest benefit.
